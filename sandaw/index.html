Academic Service

I'm actually involved in the co-organization of the 5th ASF GDR Winter school in distributed systems as well as serving as co-chair on the M4IoT workshop (2019,2018,2017) collocated with ACM/IFIP/USENIX Middleware conference. I also served in the Technical Program Committee for DFSD (2020, 2019) as well as Shadow PC member for Eurosys (2019, 2018).

I've been a visiting researcher at Università della Svizzera Italiana in Switzerland during summer 2016 under the supervision of Pr. Antonio Carzaniga, working on congestion-centred distributed routing protocols.
